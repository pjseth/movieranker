# filmfavs

filmfavs is a website or app concept that lets users rank films based on their favorites.

The ranking starts with a simple rating to estimate each film's position, then lets users compare the films they previously ranked to one another to produce a definitive list of favorite films.

## Concept

- Users add movies and assign an initial rating.
- The app estimates ranking from those ratings.
- Users then compare ranked films pairwise or in groups.
- The comparison process refines the list into a final, definitive ranking of favorites.

## Product Name

The product is called **filmfavs**.

## Prototype

Open index.html in a browser to use the ilmfavs prototype. Rate the movies, compare them side by side, and see your final ranked list.
